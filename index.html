The Night Owl International Book Fair 2022

The 2022 edition of the nigt OWl bookstore's annual book fair where avid readers can find an exhibit of hundreds of thousands of books from diffrent authors all over the world, hear some of their favourite authors speak about the craft and get one of their books signed!

13.03.2022(SUN) ~ 18(FRI)

@ Olympia Conference Centre, Hammersmith Road, Kensington, London W14 8UX, United Kingdom
